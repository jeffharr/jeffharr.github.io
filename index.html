About

Jeff Harr is a strategist and consultant who builds the things he wishes existed: games with real systems under the hood, books that argue for treating people well, and worlds detailed enough to get lost in.

The throughline is design with intention. Make the thing on purpose, for the people who'll use it, and let them own the result.
